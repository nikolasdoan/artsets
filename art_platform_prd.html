Risks & Mitigations
Demo looks like NFT speculation
The investment angle may trigger NFT associations which damage credibility with traditional collectors.
Mitigation: Feature "Certificate of Authenticity" prominently. Use the word "curator" not "token". Show physical work stories & provenance.
Mistaken for a gallery listing site
Without the investor dashboard early in the flow, stakeholders may not grasp the fintech positioning.
Mitigation: Always open the demo at the Investor Dashboard when pitching to investors. Lead with the money, not the art.
Market perceived as too niche (Vietnam only)
SEA-first beachhead may appear limiting to global investors.
Mitigation: Homepage shows works from Vietnam, Thailand, Indonesia, Philippines. Regional breadth from day one — not just Vietnamese art.
Trust mechanism not believable in a mock
A mocked "Certificate of Authenticity" may feel thin without real backend verification.
Mitigation: Design the certificate PDF template professionally. Include mock digital signatures, QR codes, curator photo. The PDF is a key trust prop.
